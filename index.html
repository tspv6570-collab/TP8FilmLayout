📄 The Paragraphs

D

The acting is strong, especially in the scenes between Erik and his sister. However, some moments feel a little slow, and viewers who prefer fast stories might lose interest.

A

"The Silent Runner" is a quiet drama directed by a new Norwegian filmmaker. This film is about Erik, a retired long-distance runner who returns to his childhood village after many years away.

E

I would recommend this film to anyone who enjoys quiet, emotional stories and beautiful natural scenery. It is a gentle film that stays in your mind after you finish watching it.

B

The story follows Erik as he reconnects with his mother, his sister, and a young neighbor. There are no big twists or action scenes — the film focuses on family problems, old memories, and slow emotional change.

C

I think the film uses the natural landscape very well. The snowy mountains and frozen lakes help show Erik's feelings, and the simple music fits the calm, thoughtful style of the film.

🗂️ Match Each Stage

1.

Introduction

Names the film, director and main character

2.

Plot Summary

Describes what happens without spoilers

3.

Evaluation

Two paragraphs judge different aspects of the film

4.

Recommendation

Gives a verdict and says who should watch it

Introduction: This section names the film, tells us who directed it, and introduces the main character.
Plot Summary: This section explains what the story is about — without giving away the ending.
Evaluation: This section comments on the quality of the film. Look for TWO paragraphs that judge different aspects.
Recommendation: This section gives a final verdict and tells us who the film is for.
